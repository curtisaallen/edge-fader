Common Issues

  • Make sure your uploaded file is a PNG, JPG, JPEG, or WEBP image.
  • If the preview does not update, try uploading the image again.
  • If the image does not insert into Canva, refresh Canva and try again.
  • For best results, use high-quality images with clear subjects.

How Edge Fader Works

Upload an image, choose a fade style, adjust the feather amount, preview the result, and insert the faded image into your Canva design.
