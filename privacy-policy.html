Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Edge Fader respects your privacy. This app is designed to help Canva users create soft transparent edge fades for images.

Information We Collect

Edge Fader does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Image Processing

Images uploaded into Edge Fader are processed only for the purpose of creating the selected fade effect. The app does not store uploaded images on an external server.

Canva Platform

Edge Fader runs inside Canva and uses Canva’s app platform to insert the finished image into your design.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share user data with third parties.
